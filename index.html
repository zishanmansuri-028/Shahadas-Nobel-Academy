    🎯 What is N-SET?

    N-SET (Nobel Scholarship Entrance Test) is a merit-based scholarship exam conducted by Shahada’s Nobel Academy for students preparing for NEET, IIT-JEE & MHT-CET.

    The exam is designed to identify talented and dedicated students and reward them with scholarships and exciting rewards.

    ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

    Who can apply for N-SET?

    Students from Class 10th, 11th, 12th and NEET repeaters are eligible.

    Is N-SET compulsory?

    N-SET is mandatory for scholarship. Admission without scholarship is also available.

    What is the registration fee?

    The N-SET registration fee is ₹149 only.

    What rewards can I get?

    Top-performing students can win laptops, tablets, smart watches and more.

    How will I get my result?

    Results and scholarship details are shared during counselling session.

    Is the registration fee refundable?

    The registration fee is non-refundable under any circumstances, as it is used for exam processing and management.

    How can I apply for the N-SET exam?

    You can apply by filling out the online registration form and completing the payment process. After successful registration, you will receive confirmation from our team.
